# Google Search Console Submission Checklist

Complete these steps right after deploying your portfolio:

1. Set your production URL in `.env`:
   - `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app`
2. Redeploy the site.
3. Verify these endpoints are live:
   - `https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app/robots.txt`
   - `https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
4. Open [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
5. Add and verify your property (`https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app`).
6. Go to **Sitemaps** and submit:
   - `https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
7. Go to **URL Inspection** and request indexing for:
   - `https://wadud-shuvro-portfolio.vercel.app/`
8. Recheck indexing status after 24-72 hours.

Notes:
- Keep your profile name exactly as `Wadud Shuvro` across the website and social profiles.
- Add your portfolio URL to LinkedIn/GitHub profiles to strengthen branded ranking signals.
